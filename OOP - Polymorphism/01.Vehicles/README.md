# Vehicles

* This is a console applicationt that models 2 vehicles (a Car and a Truck) and simulates driving and refueling them.

## Example 

* Input: Car 15 0.3
 Truck 100 0.9
 4
 Drive Car 9
 Drive Car 30
 Refuel Car 50
 Drive Truck 10

* Output: Car travelled 9 km
 Car needs refueling
 Truck travelled 10 km
 Car: 54.20
 Truck: 75.00
